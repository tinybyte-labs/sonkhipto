## Introduction

TinyByte Labs LTD ("We", "Us") built the Sonkhipto app as a free application to provide you with short news summaries. Our application is available for download on both iOS and Android platforms.

This page serves as an information resource to communicate how we collect, use, and disclose your information. If you choose to use our app, Sonkhipto, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this privacy policy.

## What We Collect

The app does require certain user information for proper operation:

- Name
- Email

We collect the above information to facilitate your use of the app and provide a personalized experience. Specifically, we contain your information for social sign-ins and anonymous sign-ins and permit us to respond to your feedback if you choose to provide any. If you send us feedback via email, your email will be collected.

However, information regarding your email and name will not be disclosed to third parties reserving us as a safe third party for your information.

## Ads and Analytics

Our source of income is derived from ads that are occasionally displayed in between news articles. Additionally, we use analytics to understand where our users are coming from. This practice helps us improve user experience and relevance of the ads shown.

## Links to Other Sites

Our app incorporates the ability to open up a browser within the app for users to read more about a short news summary. Please note that this action makes you subject to the privacy policy and terms and conditions of that particular site. We do not possess any control and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. Users are advised to verify any such third-party's respective privacy policy and terms and conditions.
